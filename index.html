About Me

Hi, my name is Reagan Vu! I am student studying Computer Science and Software Engineering at the University of Washington Bothell. After being a blind user of technology for many years, in high school I started to question and wonder about how the ubiquitous elements of modern technology, like computers, the internet, and networks worked behind the scenes.

Ever since then, I have worked to fill in the gaps in my knowledge. From pursuing my degree, to participating in hackathons, I have continued to hone my skills in a variety of topics. I believe the challenges I've overcome and my desire to improve and learn will allow me to be a meaningful contributor to any team.
